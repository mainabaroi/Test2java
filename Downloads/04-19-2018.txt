Jira - Issue & project tracking tool.

Epic: Epic describes the whole system.

Story: 

- Story in Jira means Features of the application or parts of Epic.

- Developer team creates it.

- In agile(scrum) to work with Jira we first need to create a story.

How to setup Jira Project:

On the left bottom corner click on the menu icon>> Click On Jira>> from top selections click on Projects>> From top right click on Create Project>>From Template section click on change>>Choose the first one which says Scrum.

As a tester you have to fill out the following fields in JIRA

i) Priority

ii) Issue

iii) Assignee 

iv) Sprint
